project - Movement Detroit Commission


in July 2023, i was contacted by the publicity team at Movement Detroit, an annual music festival in Detroit, to create a promotional video for the next festival.


i worked closely with my contact at Movement Detroit to iterate on and improve the result, incorporating lots of feedback.


through the course of the project, i learned Davinci Resolve for editing from scratch, i got to exercise my hard-surface modelling skills, and i had fun creating retro wireframe visuals for various different elements.

there were quite a few pieces of 3D work that were cut in the final edit but it was all good practice.
