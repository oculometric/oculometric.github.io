Staffordshire University

i'm currently studying Computer Games Programming at Staffordshire University.

Gap Year (2022-2023)

after my A levels, at the last minute i decided to take a gap year to explore my art interests. i spent the year practising with digital 3D media (specifically Blender), developing my interest in rendering and shading.

i also took a Foundation Drawing course with the OCA, which greatly improved my ability to visualise and express ideas, as well as being extremely useful for general artistic principles of proportion, presentation, tone, etc which i continue to draw on.

late into my gap year i decided to reapply to university, cancelling my originally accepted offer for Computer Science and AI at Bath and applying instead for games design and programming courses.
